Who we are

We are a team of talented and passionate techies that teach young guys the fundamentals of computer science and robotics. All of our team members have either a background in STEM related fields or computer hobbysists. We go to registered schools and engage kids in 2 hour programing lessons using interactive software and kits.

We have a tailored curriculum targeting kids from 10yrs to 16yrs touching several languages like Python, Java, HTML&CSS and Javascript. The org was formed because of our understanding of the current trends in STEM and the lack of prerequisite programming knowledge in Kenyan schools.

Our mission

●

To reach out to as many schools and kids in Kenya to create interest in them to pursue STEM courses in the future. Through our program, we aim to also mentor the kids on career paths and soft skills.
